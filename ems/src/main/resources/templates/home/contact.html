FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from candidates

How do I apply for a job? expand_more
Go to the Jobs page, find a position you're interested in, and click "Apply". You'll need to fill in your personal information and upload your CV. The whole process takes about 5 minutes.
How can I track my application? expand_more
After submitting your application, you'll receive a tracking token via email. You can use this token in the "Track Application" feature (available in the top navigation bar) to check your application status at any time.
What file formats are accepted for CV upload? expand_more
We accept CV files in PDF, DOC, and DOCX formats. The file size limit is 10 MB. We recommend PDF format to ensure your formatting is preserved.
How long does the recruitment process take? expand_more
Our typical recruitment process involves CV screening (3–5 business days), technical/role-specific interview, and final interview with the hiring manager. The full process usually takes 2–4 weeks depending on the role.
Can I apply for multiple positions? expand_more
Yes, you can apply for multiple open positions. Each application is reviewed independently. We recommend tailoring your cover letter and highlighting relevant experience for each specific role.
Do you offer remote work options? expand_more
Many of our roles offer hybrid or fully remote options. The work arrangement for each position is detailed in the job description. Our engineering and product teams are particularly flexible with remote work.
